Refund Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2025

Summary: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • 7-day window: Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • First purchase only: Refunds are limited to the first license purchase per customer.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at CDriveCleaner@proton.me with:

  • Your order number (provided by Gumroad)
  • The email address used for the purchase
  • Brief reason for the refund request (optional but appreciated)

3. Refund Processing

Once your refund is approved:

  • The refund will be processed through Gumroad within 5-10 business days
  • Your Pro license will be deactivated
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method

4. Exceptions

Refunds may not be granted in the following cases:

  • Requests made more than 7 days after purchase
  • Repeat refund requests from the same customer
  • Refund requests for free version downloads (no purchase required)

5. Contact

For refund requests or questions, email us at: CDriveCleaner@proton.me

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.
